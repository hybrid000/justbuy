Nothing Phone (1) is Everything! | About Nothing Phone (1)

By: Sushant Uploaded on 16th August 2022



Yes, this is all about Nothing phone . The first phone from the startup that gave the Nothing Ear 1 earbuds in 2021 a big step into establishing their own ecosystem that they are building And it looks familiar but also very different.

So we live in an age right now where so many of the top phones,the most popular phones are designed to appeal to the masses, That’s the point of them. So they all end up kind of looking basically the same, all very similar. Like big screen upon the front, couple cameras on the back, a couple pale colours, nothing to crazy, just a glass slab.

So if a startup were to come along and make another average phone, it would probably fit in pretty nice but there would not really be any reason to switch to it, to buy that one. So what Nothing has done is they have made a pretty unique – looking phone that also happen’s to have a bunch of cool features .

The way that really standout is with the lights given on the back. So the phone has what we would basically call the ultimate notification LED. So the notifications LED on the back of a phone has been a dying breed lately. You don’t see them very much but when you do, which is rarly, its so nice to just have like a subtle little glowing LED that just blinks to know a new message,phone call or a tweet. And this is sort of a exaggerated all-out versions of LED. So there are just over 900 LED’s making up a pattern across the whole back of this phone, around the dual cameras, around the wireless charging pad in the middle, and then some stripes up into the corner, and then down by the charging port at the bottom, and they do a great job frosting over them, so the glow really cleanly appears as just a solid continuos light bar. So this LED notification is know as glyph interface. Nothing phone (1) will have full alluminum body at the back.

Phone Features

The display and the rear panel could be protected by Gorilla Glass. In front side of the phone you will get 16 megapixel front camera, 50 megapixel + 16 megapixel rear Camera.It will have 6.55 inch OLED Display with support up to a 120Hz adaptive refresh rate.

Phone will have RAM of 8 GB, Storage of 128 GB, OS – Android 12, 4500mah of Battery Capacity, Resolution of 1080×2400 pixels,Snapdragon 778G+ SoC, 50 megapixel dual rear camera setup and 6.55 inch OLED Display with 120Hz refresh rate.

“Find Everything in Nothing”
